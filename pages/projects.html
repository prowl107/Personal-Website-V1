Arduino Lightsaber

The Arduino Lightsaber is a custom made and designed lightsaber replica using an Arduino Nano to control 288 LEDs and an unlimited amount of sound fonts.

  • Powered by Arduino Nano Every microcontroller
  • Designed all physical components and handle in Autodesk Fusion 360
  • Using 2x ws2812 RGB LED strips contained in polycarbonate blade
  • 3D printed handle in Nylon 12 through Xometry Service
  • User-programmable sounds and on the fly color changing

Project Spectre - Software Lead

Project Spectre is an active stabilized rocket designed by my university's Experimental Rocket Propulsion Lab (ERPL). My role is developing the Spectre Module which is comprised of the hardware and software that will accomplish the active stabilization.

  • Developing an active stabilization unit using an Arduino Nano Every and servos to actuate canards
  • Created wiring diagrams and documentation for hardware integration
  • Tested different electronic components such as IMUs, servos, breakout boards, and microcontrollers to verify the functionaility and effectiveness
